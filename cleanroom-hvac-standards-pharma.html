What defines a Pharma Cleanroom?

A pharma cleanroom is categorized based on the number of particles per cubic meter of air. Standards like ISO 14644 determine the classification. Frequently Asked Questions

What ISO standards are required for pharma cleanrooms?

Pharma cleanrooms typically follow ISO 14644 standards, with Class 5 to Class 8 being the most common depending on the production stage.

How does Aaron Air Care ensure cleanroom compliance?

Controlling Humidity and Temperature

Many pharmaceutical compounds are highly sensitive to moisture. Precise humidity control (often below 40% RH) is required to ensure product stability. Our turnkey solutions integrate dehumidifiers and precision cooling units with the central HVAC to provide a stable environment regardless of the outside weather in Uttar Pradesh.
